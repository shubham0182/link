About Shortly

Simple, fast, and private URL shortening

Shortly is a professional URL shortener built with modern web technologies. All your links are stored locally in your browser — nothing is sent to any server. We believe in privacy-first tools that just work.

With support for 20+ domain extensions, custom aliases, QR codes, and full history management, Shortly gives you everything you need to create and manage short links like a pro.
